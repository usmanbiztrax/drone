System Overview

The Biztrax AI Drone System is a fully autonomous aerial platform designed for intelligent, hands-free operation. It conducts missions without human intervention and features a self-charging mechanism using a custom-built landing box. When the battery is low, the drone automatically lands, recharges, and prepares for its next mission. This system pushes the boundaries of autonomous technology, offering significant potential across public safety, defense, logistics, and environmental monitoring.
